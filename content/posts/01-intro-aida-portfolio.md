As part of the introduction to AI-driven applications, we were introduced to the concept of Large Language Models (LLMs) and their role in modern software development.

From my understanding, an LLM is a model trained on large amounts of text data, which enables it to predict and generate human-like language.

Through this course, I expect to learn how AI can be integrated into software solutions and how LLMs can be used as part of a structured development process. This portfolio will document that learning journey through reflections and projects.
