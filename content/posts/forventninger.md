## My expectations

In this post, I write about my expectations for the elective course.

I hope to learn new ways of working with digital tools, reflections, and creative processes. I also want to develop my understanding through the 13 course sessions and document my progress along the way.

## What I hope to gain

I hope this course will help me strengthen both my practical and reflective skills. I want to become better at connecting theory, ideas, and my own experiences in the assignments.
